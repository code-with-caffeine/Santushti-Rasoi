Our Story

A Legacy Born in Jamshedpur

In 2014, our founders set up a modest halwai shop in the heart of Jamshedpur. What began with a single copper kadhai and a handful of recipes inherited from his grandfather has grown into Jharkhand's most trusted name in artisan sweets.

Every mithai we craft carries the weight of that original promise — only the finest ingredients, no shortcuts, no compromises. We still use stone-ground flours, single-origin saffron from Kashmir, and Anand's A2 desi ghee. Three generations later, the kadhai still simmers at dawn.
